## Judging Rubric

Each team will be evaluated on 4 dimensions. Judges will provide written feedback to every team.

|Criteria |Weight |Description |
|--|--|---------|
|Business Value |30%| Does this agent solve a real problem that businesses would pay for? Is the value proposition clear and compelling? |
|Output Quality |30%| How good is the actual work the agent produces? Accuracy, depth, usefulness. |
|Innovation |20%| Is the approach creative or differentiated? Does it stand out? |
|Long-term Potential |20%| Can this agent keep delivering value after the hackathon? Is it repeatable? |
